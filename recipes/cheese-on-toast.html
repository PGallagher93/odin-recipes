Cheese on toast

Description

The classic english meal, a bit of cheese on a bit of toast

If you're feeling fancy you can even put tomatoes on top!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 250g Mature cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. place bread under grill and begin lightly toasting
  2. slice the cheese up into 6 slices
  3. Once the bread is toasted enough add three slices to each piece and place back under the grill
  4. cook until cheese is lightly browned
