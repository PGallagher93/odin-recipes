Ham Sandwich

Description

The staple of any school child or working man's lunch

A couple slices of ham in some bread, easy to make, easy to eat. If simplicity and effectiveness is what you're after then this is the one for you.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of ham
  • 4 slices of bread
  • Margarine spread

Steps

  1. Spread the margarine on the bread
  2. add two slices of ham to two slices of bread
  3. build the sandwich
